Intro

What is DesignJam?

DesignJam services and tools are co-creative, design-fueled & human-centred. We bring practical, hands-on learning experiences to help you create a business, or make business more creative.

BigTent Events

Big Tent

BigTent Events

A ‘big tent’ jamboree, this all-day free event gathers over one hundred design leaders & learners, sharing ideas & tools while working to understand and address a complex problem. Nicknamed "TrafficJam," our first event illuminates opportunities & challenges of traffic and transit in our region. March 28, 2015 at MaRS in downtown Toronto.
